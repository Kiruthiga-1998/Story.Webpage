FULL STOCK DEVELOPEMENT


As full stack developers are responsible for both the front-end and back-end components of an application, they ensure a seamless integration between the user interface and the underlying back-end logic. They define and clarify the data architecture and establish communication protocols across the front and back ends.

FEATURES


Full-stack developers have a high level of competency in both frontend and backend languages. They are skilled in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, NodeJS, ExpressJS, Django, and PHP. Equally, full-stack developers understand various database management systems (DBMS) like MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and more..
